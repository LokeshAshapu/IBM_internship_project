Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! Cancel your premium subscription at any time with no questions asked. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

Absolutely! Start your 14-day free trial with no credit card required. Try all premium features risk-free and cancel anytime.

What happens to my profile after cancellation?

Your profile remains intact but reverts to free tier features. You keep your QR code and can still search for profiles.

Yes, you can upgrade at any time. Your billing will adjust and you'll receive a prorated credit or charge depending on your plan.

All data is encrypted and stored securely. You have complete control over access codes, and sharing is opt-in only.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and debit cards through our Stripe payment processor.
